United by a love for reading, Bakkcover brings Contributors and Adopters together to find a new home for unwanted novels

Contributors

Simply list any title along with a corresponding photo, and it'll automatically be added to our library, along with a synopsis of the book. When someone is ready to adopt your book, they will be in touch with you!

Adopters

See a book you like? Simply add it to your cart and check out - we'll send you the contact information for that contributor, so you can arrange for pick up at your convenience!
